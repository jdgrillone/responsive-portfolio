About Me

I am currently working as a CNC Machinist / Programmer. I have been in the machining industry for 4 years. However, I realized that it was not where my heart was. Computer Science and programming has always been a passion of mine. So in hopes of taking that passion to the next step, I enrolled in The Coding Boot Camp through The University of Utah Professional Education. When I am not working, I am traveling, playing music (guitar), cooking, or playing video games.
